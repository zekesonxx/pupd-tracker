Road remains closed, boil order in place due to water leak near Hilltop Apartments

February 3, 2023

Water service has been restored to buildings 1 through 22 in Hilltop Apartments, but a boil order remains in place due to a water leak in the area.

Additionally, Ross-Ade Drive remains closed from Tower Drive north to the southbound split at building 13. It will remain closed until further notice. Tower Drive between Steven Beering Drive and North Martin Jischke Drive reopened to vehicular traffic Feb. 2.

Details at https://www.purdue.edu/administrative-operations/news/2023/020223-water-leak-impacts.php

Campus is under normal operations
