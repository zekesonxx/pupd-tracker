ALERT TEST: No action necessary

January 27, 2022

This is the semi-annual TEST of the Purdue emergency warning notification system. This test simulates a level 1 incident. No action is necessary.

The Purdue University 2021 Annual Security and Fire Safety Report is now available. This report is required by federal law and contains policy statements and crime statistics for Purdue University West Lafayette campus. The policy statements address the university’s policies, procedures and programs concerning safety and security. Three years’ worth of statistics are included for certain types of crimes that were reported to have occurred on campus, in or on off-campus buildings or property owned or controlled by the university and on public property within or immediately adjacent to the campus. The most up to date report is available online at: https://www.purdue.edu/ehps/police/reports/YourCampus.pdf.

You may also request a paper copy from the Purdue University Police Department.

If you have been identified as a Campus Security Authority, please visit: https://www.purdue.edu/ethics/reporting/clery/index.php for more information and training. Lastly, please review your specific Building Emergency Plan (https://www.purdue.edu/ehps/emergency_preparedness/emergency/building-plan.html) and the Emergency Procedures Guide (https://www.purdue.edu/ehps/emergency_preparedness/flipchart/index.html) to review emergency response and evacuation/shelter procedures.
