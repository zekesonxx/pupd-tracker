Campus community must move on-street vehicles for snow removal by 11 p.m. today (Feb. 1)

Purdue University and West Lafayette police are urging students, staff and faculty on the West Lafayette campus to move their vehicles from street parking on and around the Purdue University campus ahead of potentially significant weather events this week. Moving vehicles will facilitate snow removal efforts over the coming days.

More details are at: https://www.purdue.edu/newsroom/releases/2022/Q1/campus-community-to-move-on-street-vehicles-for-snow-removal-by-11-p.m.-today-feb.-1-at-risk-of-towing.html

Campus is under normal operations
